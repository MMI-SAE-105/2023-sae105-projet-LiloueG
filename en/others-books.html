Other books by the author

Here are the different James Dashner books in the same saga:

Le Labyrinthe (2012)

Thomas wakes up in an elevator that transports him to a giant labyrinth. He is surrounded by other young people, all with amnesia. They don't know who they are or how they got there. The young people, nicknamed the Blocards, are forced to live in the labyrinth. They must find a way to escape before the Creatures, monsters that attack them at night, kill them. Thomas proves to be a natural leader. He's determined to find a way out of the labyrinth and discover the truth about their situation. After many attempts, Thomas and the other Blocards discover a way out of the labyrinth. They escape and discover a post-apocalyptic world.

Le Labyrinthe, La Terre brûlée (2013)

Thomas and the other labyrinth survivors escape the labyrinth and find themselves in a post-apocalyptic world. They are pursued by WICKED, the organization that trapped them in the labyrinth. Thomas and his friends must find a way to survive in this hostile world and discover the truth about WICKED. They travel to a ruined city, where they meet other survivors. They learn that WICKED is an organization that conducts experiments on young people. Thomas and his friends must find a way to thwart WICKED's plans and save the world.
